About Me

Shawn Foster

UX/UI Developer

I'm a UX/UI Developer and Product Designer, with a background in Business Administration. As a Web Developer, it quickly became apparent to me, with the advancement and subsequent oversaturation of Web & Mobile technology, a focus on the user was the only way to stay relevant in the industry.  The future of technology depends on what more empathy and understanding of their users. With this knowledge, I became a UX-centric Developer and later worked entirely in UX/UI Design as well, which has brought me to where I am today, a UX/UI Developer.

I believe that the joining of both practices has allowed me to better create new and exciting experiences for people. Every person is responsible for what they put out in the world. I ensure that my designs resonate with people they come in contact with by fusing an empathic thought process and practical design models, to better serve the user.
